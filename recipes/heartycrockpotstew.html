Hearty Crockpot Beef Stew

Description

A hearty beef and vegetable stew with the convenience of a slow cooker!

This is a wholesome stew featuring a few basic ingredients including beef, potatoes, carrots, peas and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef cubes
  • 5 large potatoes (peeled & quartered)
  • 5 carrots (peeled)
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of peas (frozen or canned)
  • Concentrated cube of beef flavor
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • (Optional) 1 teaspoon of chilli peppers for some heat!
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Add all of the above ingredients above to your slow cooker and cook roughly 6 hours... That's it!
