Shepherd's Pie

Description

A true classic! This Shepherds's pie recipe is simple to make yet very tasty!

All you need are a few staples mentioned below and you are ready to create this hearty dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 6 large potatoes (peeled)
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of chilli peppers
  • 1 teaspoon of parsley

Steps

  1. In a skillet or frying pan, cook ground beef under medium heat, adding salt, pepper, chilli peppers and mushrooms until beef is fully cooked.
  2. Boil potatoes until soft for mashing and drain water.
  3. Mash/mix potatoes in a large bowl along with butter and milk.
  4. Using an oven safe casserole or glass dish, spread the ground beef mixture.
  5. Next add the can of corn (drained) to make an even layer above the beef mixture.
  6. Finally layer the mashed potatoes evenly above the corn.
  7. Sprinkle parsley on top
  8. Bake in oven at 350 fahrenheit for 45 minutes
  9. Let cool for 10 minutes and enjoy!
