Simple Quesadillas

Description

Who doesn't like some delicious cheesy quesadillas!?

At its core, all you really need are tortillas and cheese of your choice. the beauty of quesadillas is that you can add so many different garnishes to suit your taste!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (as many as you like)
  • Any cheese you want cut in 1/4 inch strips
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1 avocado in slices

Steps

  1. Place a pan on stovetop over medium heat.
  2. Add as many tortillas you can fit on the pan and place 1-2 strips of sliced cheese in the center of each.
  3. Cook until cheese is mostly melted and remove tortillas from the pan.
  4. Use your remaining ingredients (lettuce, tomato, avocado) to garnish each tortilla.
  5. Fold tortillas in half and serve!
  6. (Optional) add some hot sauce for extra zip!
